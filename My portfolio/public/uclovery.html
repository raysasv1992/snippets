Who will use this extension?

People who want to earn Bitcoin free and who want to check its live price constantly

What this extension will do?

It will allow people to check the live prices of bitcoin and other main cryptocurrencies by only opening a new tab. They will be capable of winning Bitcoin back by using it.

When will this extension be used?

Everytime a person opens a new tab at work, home or other place.

Why will users use this extension?

Because they will be able to earn Bitcoin back, check cryptocurrencies prices and get styled tabs in their browser.

Problem:

Nowadays, the only ways to earn Bitcoin is by buying it or mining it. Besides, Cryptocurrencies enthusiasts have to constantly visit other sites to check their current prices.

Goal:

To create a better browsing experience where users will be able to check Bitcoin and other main Cryptocurrencies live prices while earning Bitcoin back by browsing.

My Solution:

The Design of a Chrome Extension which will allow users to enjoy a greater browsing experience with high-quality background images, showing live prices of Bitcoin and mayor Cryptocurrencies and giving them Bitcoin back every time they open a new tab through the earnings of Ads.
