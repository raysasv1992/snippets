Roar Bikes

Project:

Creation of a website for an e-commerce store.

Client:

Small and local store of contemporary bicycles sold exclusively from their own website. Roar Bikes currently has 3 models of bike to purchase (Siamese, Sphynx, Bengal).

Primary objective:

To build an e-commerce website for people to browse & purchase.

The Process

Our Persona

Responsive Design

A good UI UX designer has always to think how to scale their product and how to make it responsive-friendly. Even though, if it's not a project's current requirement it's a good practice because in the future your design may need to be adapted to a new device and this can be very useful.

This project allowed me to start thinking responsively since the beginning. Since the desktop sketches I started creating responsive elements capable of later been adapted to a mobile platform.

I like to design taking into account my product's responsiveness and since I know how to code it in HTML and CSS or SASS, I am able to think in advance if my design will be feasible to create.

I like to imagine if a set of columns will migrate to a set of rows in a mobile platform, like in this case.

UI Design System

Design Systems are projects themselves and they allow us to create a visual language which maintains and establishes harmony in our product.
They are essential guidelines that continue evolving throughout the project's life and they are a mean of communication and understanding for every member of the team, investors, clients, designers and developers.

Desktop Wireframes

Login Page

Home Page

The Home Page includes all the client's requirements: a Nav Bar showing the Logo, a Shopping Basket and Login and Contact options.

It has a section featuring the Three Different Bike Models the Company sells and another one with Owner Photos from owner's instagram accounts.

At the end it has the footer with the store basic information and their social media channels.

Specific Bike Product Page

This Product Pages include all the client's requirements: a photograph of the bike, its description, price, available colours and an Add to Cart option.

It was required to add owner's photos and I maintained the home page's style but in this section only the selling bike is featured.
