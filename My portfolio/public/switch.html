This is a company who wants to re-brand, improve user experience and visually modernise their website. It is an online CV builder. Their motto is: "Increase your chance of getting a job by creating your CV with our professionally-designed CV templates. It is a B2C service providing cv creation tools, templates and best practices that help individuals land their new job.

Data:
  • Country: United States
  • Language: English (they have multiple web domains, with multiple languages)
  • Employees: 25
  • Known funding: Privately funded

Quick analytics breakdown

Summary of their google analytics report can be found below.

Age

Most sites see majority of traffic from the 24-34 age range, typically followed closely by the 18-24 and then 35-44 age ranges.

  1. 25-34 (27%)
  2. 18-24 (21%)
  3. 35-44 (19%)
  4. 45-54 (16%)
  5. 55-64 (10%)
  6. 65+ (5%)

Gender

Slight majority of overall traffic is male, though traffic with conversions tends to balance out to be more evenly male/female.

  1. Male (52%)
  2. Female (48%)

Technology

Seems that majority of users tend to come through desktop devices.

All Users:

  1. Desktop (52%)
  2. Mobile (39%)
  3. Tablet (7%)

Final Observations:

For instance, we can see that 52% of our audience is male, this means we won’t be using too much pink. Majority of our audience is pretty “youngish” as 48% users are under 34 years old. This means we can be modern, clean and precise in our aesthetic. We can be “smart” rather than “familiar” with some of our functions and styling choices.
